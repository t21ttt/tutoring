Who we are & What we do?

At Ethio-kokeb Tutors, we believe parents who want to hire tutors or students who seek tutoring should get an easy and convenient access to find top quality tutors. Hence, we have build the first online platform in Ethiopia that connects parents/students who seek tutoring service with top tutors based on their needs.
